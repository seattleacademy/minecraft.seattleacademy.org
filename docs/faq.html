Enable Night Vision
To avoid needing to add lighting to the rooms, you can issue the command:
/effect give @p night_vision 99999 255 true
To remove the effect, enter /effect clear @p minecraft:night_vision This is the general form of the command /effect <player> <effect> [seconds] [amplifier] [hideParticles]

    • Map Art in Minecraft
    For additional detail on walls like room numbers, whiteboards, or even computer screens, map art is a good solution. Map art works by taking a 128x128 area or larger, and using minecraft's map item to map it out. This essentially gives the user a 128x128 pixelart canvas. Here's a Reddit post with a tutorial, explanation, and software for creating map art. https://www.reddit.com/r/Minecraft/comments/5w6dvl/map_art_tutorial_for_your_survival_worlds/
